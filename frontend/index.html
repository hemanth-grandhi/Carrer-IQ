About career-IQ

Many candidates fail to get roles because:

  • Their resume does not match the role
  • Their skills are outdated
  • They do not know what skills are required for specific roles

career-IQ solves this problem using AI by:

  • Analyzing resumes - Get a comprehensive analysis of your resume and identify areas for improvement
  • Identifying skill gaps - See exactly what skills you're missing compared to job requirements
  • Suggesting role-specific skills - Discover the exact skills needed for your target role
  • Providing a clear roadmap to crack the job - Receive personalized guidance and step-by-step plans to achieve your career goals
